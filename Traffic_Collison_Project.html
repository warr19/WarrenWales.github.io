San Diego Traffic Collisions Resulting in Deaths

Each point is representative of a traffic collision recorded by the San Diego police department resulting in 1 or more deaths. This visualization covers data ranging from 2015 to 2023. Smaller size and blue color represent fewer deaths having occurred at that point. Larger size and red color represent more deaths having occurred at that point.

Upon further inspection one can see that no data points appear in the city of Chula Vista. This leads me to believe that Chula Vista reports their traffic incidents seperately from the rest of San Diego county.
